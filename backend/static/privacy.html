Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026
⚠️ This is a starting template, not finalized legal advice. Have it reviewed by a qualified lawyer for your jurisdiction before relying on it.

1. What we store

  • Account data: your email address and a securely hashed password (argon2 — we never store your password in plain text).
  • Your API keys and social tokens: the third-party keys you add (OpenRouter, Instagram, imgbb, X, stock photo services). These are encrypted at rest and used only to run your own generation and publishing. We never display them back to you in full or share them.
  • Content you create: the posts, captions, images, and schedules you generate, plus basic usage/cost records for your account.

2. How we use it

Your data is used only to operate the Service for you: generating content with your keys, publishing to the networks you connect, showing your own posts and analytics, and keeping your account working. We do not sell your data.

3. Third parties

When you generate or publish, your content and the relevant keys are sent to the third-party services you chose (for example OpenRouter, Instagram/Meta, imgbb, X). Their handling of that data is governed by their own privacy policies. Verification and password-reset emails are sent through our email provider.

4. Data isolation

Accounts are isolated: your posts, keys, and usage are visible only to your account, not to other users.

5. Security

Keys are encrypted at rest, traffic is served over HTTPS, and access requires authentication. No system is perfectly secure; you are responsible for keeping your own login safe. If you believe your account or keys are compromised, rotate the affected keys on the third-party platform and contact us.

6. Retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can remove stored keys at any time in Settings (which clears them from our storage). To delete your account and associated data, contact us.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: cryptodevonblock@gmail.com.
